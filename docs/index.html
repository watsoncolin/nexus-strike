How to Play

TOUCH Touch and hold anywhere on screen - your ship follows your finger
TAP Tap to fire bullets (automatic while moving)
COLLECT Touch power-up icons to activate special abilities
SURVIVE Avoid enemy contact and destroy as many enemies as possible
